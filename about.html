--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About

Founded in 1996, Soundbytes was the first secular co-ed a cappella group on Carnegie Mellon University’s campus.

Known for their fierce color schemes and sultry style, Soundbytes pride themselves on their infectious performance groove - bringing their passion and sense of fun to any song that they perform. Soundbytes boast of notable alumni like James Wolpert, a top 5 contestant on NBC’s The Voice Season 5, and Russ Kaplan, who is one of the composers of the A Cappella Broadway musical, In Transit.
